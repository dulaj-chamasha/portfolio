About Me

Hello! I'm Dulaj, an Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering graduate with a passion for IoT systems, firmware development, and VLSI design. I love building smart devices that solve real‑world problems.

My interests include embedded systems, FPGA/ASIC design, and developing assistive technology such as my ongoing smart wearable for the deaf.

Outside work, you'll find me exploring new tech trends or sharing knowledge on my YouTube channel.
